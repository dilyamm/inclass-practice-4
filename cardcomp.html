GUTS

Guts is the second studio album by American singer and songwriter Olivia Rodrigo. It was released on September 8, 2023, through Geffen Records. It was written and recorded with Rodrigo working closely alongside Dan Nigro, the producer and multi-instrumentalist of her debut album Sour.
